[Sivunvaihto_1_{9,}]
The aforementioned transmission line owner and landowner have entered into the following agreement concerning
permanent restriction of the right of use pertaining to the landowner’s aforementioned property and the
compensation payable thereon for the purpose of constructing and maintaining a network. This contract template is
based on section 161 of the Land Use and Building Act (1999/132).
[Sivunvaihto_1_{7,8}]
1
The transmission line owner shall, while taking due account of the landowner’s wishes to the extent possible, have
the right to:
  • Overhead line:
  • a)
    construct and maintain in the aforementioned property the transmission lines, transformer and switching
    substations, columns and any equipment, open-wire transmission lines and overhead cables mounted
    thereon specified in the enclosed compensation calculation and/or map annex. The transmission line
    owner shall have the right to add new transmission lines and other structures in the existing columns
    insofar as this will not cause any further harm to the landowner;
    • [Sivunvaihto_1_{6,6}]
  • b)
    make minor changes to the planned locations of the aforementioned transmission lines, substations
    and structures that may be warranted by control measurements;
    • [Sivunvaihto_1_{5,5}]
  • c)
    access the property for the purpose of carrying out planning, marking, construction,
    maintenance and operating actions on the transmission lines, substations and structures;
    • [Sivunvaihto_1_{4,4}]
  • d)
    remove any trees, shrubs and brushes from the area as deemed necessary in view of the construction work
    during construction of the network.
    Any trees or shrubs planted in the curtilage shall be preserved to the extent possible;
    • [Sivunvaihto_1_{3,3}]
  • e)
    keep the transmission line areas free of trees and, where necessary, of shrubs and brushes, as
    required in view of the operational reliability of the transmission lines and the applicable electrical
    safety regulations;
    • [Sivunvaihto_1_{2,2}]
  • f)
    remove, in the event of disruptions, any trees outside the transmission line area that have fallen or bent
    over the transmission lines or otherwise endanger the distribution of electricity;
    • [Sivunvaihto_1_{1,1}]
  • g)
    remove any small-topped and slender trees outside the transmission line area or other individual trees that
    may cause damage to the transmission line during strong winds or due to a heavy snow load, for example.
    This is subject to the precondition that the transmission line owner notifies the landowner well in advance in
    writing or by other appropriate means of the clearings, asking the landowner to contact the transmission
    line owner in the event that the landowner wants to first agree on any particulars related to the removal of
    the trees. Any harm caused to the landowner as a result of this shall be separately agreed upon as
    provided in section 5 hereof.
    • [Sivunvaihto_2_{10,};
    ]
  • Ground cable
  • a)
    construct and maintain in the aforementioned property the cables, transformer and switching substations,
    pipings and any marking devices and distribution cabinets specified in the enclosed compensation
    calculation and/or map annex;
  • b)
    make minor changes to the planned locations of the aforementioned cables, substations and
    structures that may be warranted by control measurements;
    • [Sivunvaihto_2_{9,9};
    ]
  • c)
    remove any trees, shrubs and brushes from the area as deemed necessary in view of the construction
    work during construction of the network. Any trees or shrubs planted in the curtilage shall be preserved to
    the extent possible;
    • [Sivunvaihto_2_{7,8};
    ]
  • d)
    carry out any maintenance work later on that is deemed necessary in view of servicing the cable;
  • e)
    access the property for the purpose of carrying out planning, marking, construction, maintenance and
    operating actions on the cables and structures;
  • f)
    place in the transmission line area additional cables and structures within the meaning of point a) above
    provided that this will not alter the width of the transmission line area. The transmission line owner
    undertakes to notify the landowner of any such addition of cables and structures. Any harm caused as a
    result of such addition shall be subject to the provisions of
    section 5 hereof.
[Sivunvaihto_2_{5,6};
]
The location of the transmission line area is defined in the enclosed map (Annex 1). The width of the
transmission line area is defined in the enclosed compensation calculation (Annex 2), section “basis of the
compensation calculation” / in the map annex.
2
Any trees, brushes and shrubs felled from the transmission line area shall remain the landowner’s property that is to
be removed from the site. If the trees remain the transmission line owner’s property, they shall be compensated
according to their fair value.
[Sivunvaihto_2_{3,4};
]
3
Construction activities and the keeping of open depots and equipment in the vicinity of the transmission line are
restricted by the provisions of the Land Use and Building Act and the electrical safety regulations. More specific
instructions on the procedure to be followed in the aforementioned cases are available from the authorities and
the transmission line owner.
[Sivunvaihto_2_{2,2};
]
For protecting the structures of the transmission line network, the landowner shall notify the transmission line
owner well in advance of any felling, blasting, ditching or other comparable work carried out on his part owing to
which the network may be subject to the risk of damage. The transmission line owner shall, at the landowner’s
request, show the exact location of the ground cable in the terrain free of charge and give instructions for
protecting the network. If the measures necessary for protecting the network are negligible, the transmission line
owner shall carry out the work at his own expense.
[Sivunvaihto_2_{1,1};
]
When the aforementioned work is carried out in the vicinity of the network structures, the landowner is required to
proceed in such a way that no damage is caused to the network structures. The transmission line owner shall
provide felling assistance where necessary. Felling assistance does not mean the actual felling work. The
landowner is required to notify the transmission line owner of the need for felling assistance no later than three
working days prior to the commencement of the felling work.
4
The transmission line owner shall pay the landowner the compensations detailed in the enclosed compensation
calculation. The compensations shall be paid as a lump sum within one (1) month of the commencement of the
construction work.
Total amount of compensation [KorvausYht] €
[Sivunvaihto_3_{10,};
]
If the compensation is not paid within the prescribed time limit, the transmission line owner shall pay
penalty interest as defined in the Finnish Interest Act for the duration of the delay.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{9,9};
]
5
The transmission line owner is liable to repair any damage caused during construction time and in connection with
maintenance and operation actions or agree on their compensation with the landowner on a case-by-case basis.
The compensation for damage shall be paid within one (1) month of the date when the compensation amount was
agreed upon.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{7,8};
]
6
The landowner may, at his own expense, have the transmission line/structures moved to a new location
approved by the transmission line owner. The moving shall be carried out by the transmission line owner.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{6,6};
]
7
The landowner is required to disclose the content of this agreement to the usufructuary. In the event that the
landowner surrenders his right of ownership or usufruct, the landowner undertakes to inform the new owner or
usufructuary of the transmission line owner’s rights under this agreement and to have them recorded in the deed of
transfer or lease, respectively.
This agreement and the compensations paid are also binding upon any successive owner or usufructuary.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{4,5};
]
8
The transmission line owner shall have the right to surrender the right of use of the columns to another
transmission line owner. No separate compensation shall be paid to the landowner of such surrender. The name
and address of the new holder of the right of use and the period of validity of the right of use shall be notified to the
landowner where possible. The transmission line owner to whom the right of use has been surrendered shall then
have the right to access the landowner’s land for the purpose of carrying out planning, construction and
maintenance work.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{3,3};
]
9
The landowner shall have the right to exploit the transmission line area. When exploiting the transmission line
area, the landowner shall comply with the then-current electrical safety regulations. However, the growing of trees
and seedlings in the overhead line area must be separately agreed upon with the transmission line owner.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{2,2};
]
10
The transmission line owner shall be entitled to assign this agreement to another transmission line owner.
No separate compensation shall be paid to the landowner of such assignment.
[Sivunvaihto_3_{1,1};
]
11
This agreement shall become effective immediately when duly signed and remain in force until the network
structures are removed.
When the network structures have been removed, the transmission line owner shall return the transmission
line area to a condition that is appropriate in view of its former use. The landowner shall be notified of the
removal of the transmission line where possible.
12
This agreement shall lapse if the construction has not been commenced within two (2) years of the date when
the agreement was signed. If the commencement of the construction work is delayed for a special reason
beyond the transmission line owner’s control, the term of validity of the agreement shall be extended for a
maximum of one (1) year. The transmission line owner shall notify the landowner thereof well in advance, in
any event no later than prior to the expiry of the two-year time limit.
[Sivunvaihto_4_{9,};
]
13
This agreement has been executed in duplicate, with one copy for each party.
